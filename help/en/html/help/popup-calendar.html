Pop Up Calendar

The popup calendar is available within many applications where a date is required.

To select a date:
- Tap the dropdown list or date field in the application screen you are using
- Tap the Month dropdown list or use the left/right arrow buttons, or first/last buttons to select the month you require
- Tap the Year up/down arrows to select the required year
- Tap the day of the month on the popup calendar to select it

You can also tap outside the calendar to close it without changing the date.

See also Setting the Date.
